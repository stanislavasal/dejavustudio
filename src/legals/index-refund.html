Overview

Due to the instant digital delivery nature of our products, all sales are generally final. Once you have accessed the download links provided after purchase, refunds are not typically available. This policy exists because digital products cannot be "returned" in the traditional sense once downloaded.

Eligibility

Refunds may be considered only in the following exceptional circumstances: technical issues that prevent successful download within 48 hours of purchase, corrupted or significantly defective files that cannot be used as intended, duplicate purchases made in error within 24 hours, or unauthorized transactions that are reported immediately with proper documentation.

Timeframe

All refund requests must be submitted within 48 hours of the original purchase date. Requests submitted after this period will not be considered. The 48-hour window begins from the time of purchase confirmation, not from when you first attempt to download the files.

Process

To request a refund, contact our support team at [your-email@domain.com] with your order number, reason for the refund request, and detailed description of any technical issues encountered. Include screenshots or error messages if applicable. We will investigate your request and respond within 48-72 hours.

Requirements

For technical issue refunds, you must demonstrate that you attempted to download the files using different browsers and devices. You must also confirm that your system meets the minimum requirements specified on the product page. For quality issues, provide specific details about how the product fails to meet the described specifications.

Processing

Approved refunds will be processed back to your original payment method within 5-10 business days. Credit card refunds may take additional time to appear on your statement depending on your bank's processing schedule. PayPal refunds are typically processed within 24-48 hours.

Exceptions

No refunds will be provided for change of mind, buyer's remorse, incompatibility with specific software versions not listed in requirements, inability to use the product due to lack of software knowledge, or dissatisfaction with design style or quality that matches the preview shown.

Alternatives

Instead of refunds, we may offer account credit for future purchases, replacement files if originals are corrupted, or alternative product formats when available. These alternatives may be provided at our discretion and do not constitute an obligation to provide refunds in similar future cases.

Chargebacks

Initiating a chargeback or payment dispute without first contacting our support team may result in immediate account suspension and loss of access to purchased products. We maintain detailed transaction records and will contest illegitimate chargebacks through proper channels.

Partial Refunds

For bundle purchases where only some products have technical issues, partial refunds may be considered for the affected items only. The refund amount will be calculated based on the individual product values within the bundle pricing structure.

Store Credit

In some cases, we may offer store credit instead of monetary refunds. Store credit has no expiration date and can be applied to future purchases. Store credit cannot be transferred to other accounts or converted back to cash.

Contact

For questions about these terms, contact us at [your-email@domain.com], [Your Business Address], or [Your Phone Number]. We review all requests carefully and respond promptly.
