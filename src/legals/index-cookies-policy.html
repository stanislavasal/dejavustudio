This Cookies Policy explains how Deja Vu Studio uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website. We use cookies to improve your browsing experience, analyze website performance, and provide personalized content and advertisements.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, login information, and other details to make your next visit easier and more useful. Cookies can be session-based, which expire when you close your browser, or persistent, which remain on your device for a set period.

Types We Use

We use essential cookies that are necessary for our website to function properly, including shopping cart functionality and user authentication. Performance cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information about page visits and user behavior. Functional cookies remember your preferences such as language settings and display options. Marketing cookies track your browsing habits to show you relevant advertisements on our site and other websites.

Third-Party

Our website may use third-party cookies from services like Google Analytics for website analytics, payment processors for transaction processing, and advertising networks for showing relevant ads. These third parties may collect information about your online activities across different websites over time.

Managing

You can control cookies through your browser settings by accepting all cookies, rejecting all cookies, or being notified when a cookie is set. Most browsers allow you to delete existing cookies and prevent future cookies from being stored. However, disabling certain cookies may affect your ability to use some features of our website.

Browser Settings

To manage cookies in popular browsers, go to Settings or Preferences and look for Privacy or Security options. In Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge, you can find cookie settings under Privacy and Security sections. You can also use private or incognito browsing modes to prevent cookies from being stored during your session.

Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for our website to operate and cannot be disabled. They include cookies that enable you to log into secure areas, use shopping cart functionality, and maintain your session while browsing. Without these cookies, services you have requested cannot be provided.

Analytics

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors use our website, which pages are most popular, and how users navigate through our site. This information helps us improve our website design and user experience. The data collected is aggregated and anonymous.

Marketing

Marketing cookies track your browsing behavior to show you personalized advertisements based on your interests. These cookies may be set by our advertising partners and can create a profile of your interests to show relevant ads on other websites you visit.

Consent

By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. If you do not agree with our use of cookies, please adjust your browser settings or stop using our website. You can withdraw your consent at any time by changing your browser settings.

Updates

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our cookie usage or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
